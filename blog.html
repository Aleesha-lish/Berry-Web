    How to style a Quiff

    To achieve a quiff hairstyle, you typically start with clean, towel-dried hair. Apply a styling product such as mousse or gel to add texture and hold. Then, use a blow dryer and a round brush to create volume and lift at the roots. Take a section of hair at the front and comb it upward, using a comb or your fingers to create height and shape. You can use a hairdryer and comb to set the quiff in place, and finish with hairspray for added hold.
